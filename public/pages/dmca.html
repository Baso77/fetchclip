DMCA Policy

FetchClip respects intellectual property rights and responds to valid copyright claims.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

FetchClip operates as a tool to facilitate downloading of publicly accessible media. We take copyright infringement seriously and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

Our Position on Copyright

FetchClip does not host, store, or distribute any copyrighted content. We provide a technical service that allows users to download publicly available media from third-party platforms. The responsibility for complying with copyright law lies with the user.

However, we acknowledge that our service could potentially be misused and we take DMCA notices seriously.

Filing a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you believe that content accessible through FetchClip infringes your copyright, please send a written notice to our designated agent with the following information:

  • A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or their authorized representative
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed
  • Identification of the material that you claim is infringing, with information sufficient to locate it
  • Your contact information (address, phone number, and email address)
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
  • A statement that the information in the notification is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

Where to Send DMCA Notices

  • Email: dmca@fetchclip.app
  • Subject line: "DMCA Takedown Notice"

We will respond to valid DMCA notices within 5-10 business days.

Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was wrongfully removed due to a DMCA notice, you may file a counter-notice with our DMCA agent containing the required information under 17 U.S.C. § 512(g)(3).

Repeat Infringer Policy

FetchClip reserves the right to block access from any user or IP address that is subject to multiple valid DMCA notices.
