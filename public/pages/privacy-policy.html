Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's exactly how FetchClip handles your data.

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Effective immediately

Welcome to FetchClip ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how FetchClip collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website at fetchclip.app and our video downloading service.

By using FetchClip, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

FetchClip does not require account registration. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal information unless you voluntarily contact us through our contact form.

If you submit a contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message content

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use FetchClip, we automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Usage data: The URLs you submit for processing (used only to fetch the requested media)
  • Platform data: The social media platform detected from your submitted URL
  • Hashed IP address: A one-way hash of your IP address for rate limiting and abuse prevention (cannot be reversed to identify you)
  • Browser type and version (via standard HTTP headers)
  • Referring URL (if applicable)

1.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT store your downloaded videos or media files
  • We do NOT track your browsing history across other websites
  • We do NOT collect financial or payment information
  • We do NOT store raw IP addresses
  • We do NOT use third-party advertising trackers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the FetchClip video downloading service
  • Process media fetch requests you submit
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and excessive automated usage
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve the service
  • Respond to support inquiries submitted through our contact form

3. Data Storage and Retention

We use Supabase (a secure cloud database provider) to store minimal usage logs. These logs contain:

  • The social media platform requested
  • A hashed (non-reversible) identifier
  • Timestamps

Submitted URLs are retained in logs for up to 30 days for abuse prevention, then automatically deleted. Downloaded media is never stored on our servers — all media passes through our proxy temporarily and is not saved.

4. Cookies

FetchClip uses minimal cookies:

  • Session preferences: Remembering your selected platform tab and quality preference (these are stored locally in your browser, not on our servers)

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

FetchClip uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database hosting for usage analytics. Supabase's privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy
  • Google Fonts: Typography loading. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Vercel: Hosting and CDN. Subject to Vercel's privacy policy.

We do not share your personal information with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

FetchClip is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@fetchclip.app and we will promptly delete it.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, hashed identifiers instead of raw identifying data, and secure cloud storage through Supabase.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@fetchclip.app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of FetchClip after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@fetchclip.app
  • Contact form: fetchclip.app/contact
